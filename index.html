When are our Events?

Although a Gurdwara is open to all at any time of the day, we host weekly diwans (aka sermons) every Friday evening and Sunday mornings. Check out the calendar above for more events!

person_pin

Can I visit a Gurdwara?

Yes! We are open to everyone regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, or religious affiliation. Here all men, women and children are treated equal. A Gurdwara will also always offer shelter and food to anyone in need.
