Project Lrn

Providing a platform for the next generation of educational content.

Engaging

We want to "game-ify" the textbook experience. Our games will provide the same educational content, but in ways that are engaging for all.

Customizable

Learn in the way you feel most cofortable. Our platform will include content for students who like to learn by seeing, hearing, and doing.

Multi-Platform

Accessing our content is easy. The Lrn Platform will be accessible on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and computers.

About Project Lrn

Project Lrn is an initiative that was started by one individual: Gary Sahota, a full time MBA student at the Rady School of Management at the University of California, San Diego.
